## MICCAI 2017: A Survey of Deep Learning Papers

Since 1998 the MICCAI society has brought together researchers working on computational methods
to understand medical images and aid interventions.
The 20th MICCAI conference was held at Quebec City, Quebec, Canada this September.
Here, I provide an overview of the conference and a brief survey of deep learning works presented in the main conference.
